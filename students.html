Who Can Apply

Bodhaayan is for you if…

✅

You are a student from a rural, semi-urban, or marginalised urban community in India.

✅

You have genuine curiosity about how things work — in nature, technology, health, energy, or any domain.

✅

You have a question, problem, or idea you want to explore scientifically — even if you don't know where to start.

✅

You are between 14 and 22 years old and currently enrolled in school or college.

⭐

No prior research experience required. Bodhaayan is designed for beginners.
