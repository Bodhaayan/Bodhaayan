The Challenge

Talent is everywhere.
Opportunity is not.

Across India's villages and marginalized urban communities there are brilliant young minds with genuine scientific potential. But without mentors, labs, or funding — that potential quietly disappears.

"We are not losing students to a lack of ability. We are losing them to a lack of access."

Bodhaayan exists to close that gap — permanently, at scale, across every community in India.

👥

Bodhaayan Vigyan Karyshala

14 students — 7 girls and 7 boys — built a working thermoelectric chulha that converts heat from a traditional stove into electricity. Over 4 days, they experienced real experimental science for the first time.
